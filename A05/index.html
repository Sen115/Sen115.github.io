PUP Sto. Tomas, Batangas

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Branch Campus

About

PUP Sto. Tomas is one of the branches of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. It provides affordable and quality education to students in Batangas and nearby provinces.

It is known for producing competent graduates in various fields such as Information Technology, Business, and Engineering.

Programs Offered

  • BS in Information Technology
  • BS in Business Administration
  • BS in Accountancy
  • BS in Office Administration
  • BS in Business Teacher Education
  • BS in Industrial and Organizational Psychology
  • BS in Electrical Engineering
  • BS in Entrepreneurial Management

Location

Santo Tomas, Batangas, Philippines
